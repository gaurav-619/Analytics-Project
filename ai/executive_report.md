**Executive Report**

### Executive Summary

Our business performance metrics indicate a strong revenue growth, with a total revenue of $8,630,269.31 and 92,678 transactions. The top revenue channel remains unattributed, highlighting the need for further analysis. The marketing efforts have been successful, with Affiliate being the top marketing channel and generating $1,652,899.35 in revenue. However, our A/B testing results suggest that the Control variant outperformed other variants, indicating potential room for improvement.

### Sales Performance Analysis

* Total Revenue: $8,630,269.31
* Total Transactions: 92,678
* Average Order Value: $91.31
* Top Revenue Channel: Unattributed

The strong revenue growth and high transaction volume are positive indicators of the business's performance. However, the unattributed top revenue channel suggests that there may be opportunities to optimize sales performance by identifying and leveraging the most effective channels.

### Funnel Analysis

* Purchase Conversion Rate: 64.04%
* Highest Dropoff Stage: purchase

The high purchase conversion rate indicates that the business is effective in converting customers into purchases. However, the highest dropoff stage being "purchase" suggests that there may be opportunities to improve the checkout process or reduce friction.

### Experiment Analysis

* Best Variant: Control
* Best Conversion Rate: 43.07%

The Control variant outperformed other variants in the A/B testing, indicating that the current design or approach is the most effective. However, the best conversion rate of 43.07% suggests that there may be opportunities to improve the user experience or optimize the experiment design.

### Strategic Recommendations

1. **Invest in Affiliate marketing**: Continue to invest in Affiliate marketing efforts, as it has been the most successful channel.
2. **Optimize the checkout process**: Improve the checkout process or reduce friction to reduce dropoff at the "purchase" stage.
3. **Experiment with new variants**: Experiment with new variants or designs to improve the user experience and conversion rates.
4. **Analyze unattributed top revenue channel**: Investigate the unattributed top revenue channel to identify opportunities to optimize sales performance.

### Risks & Opportunities

* **Risk: Over-reliance on Affiliate marketing**: The business may become too reliant on Affiliate marketing, making it vulnerable to changes in the market or Affiliate partner performance.
* **Opportunity: Improved user experience**: Optimizing the user experience through experimentation and design improvements could lead to increased conversion rates and revenue growth.
* **Opportunity: Identifying new revenue channels**: Analyzing the unattributed top revenue channel could lead to the identification of new revenue opportunities and growth areas.
